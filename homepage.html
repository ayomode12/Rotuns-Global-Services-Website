Who Are We?

A Proven Leader in Mechanical, Engineering, and Plumbing Services.

Rotuns Global Services, LLC is a proven leader in the field of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) services. With a deep commitment to excellence and a focus on delivering high-quality solutions, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, Rotuns Global Services is a fully licensed professional engineering design firm that operates with integrity, precision, and a client-first approach. Our dedicated team of over 100 highly skilled professionals brings together years of expertise and innovation to tackle the most complex engineering challenges. Our Commitment At Rotuns Global Services, our mission is to provide cutting-edge engineering solutions that not only meet but exceed our clients' expectations. We are dedicated to designing and delivering systems that are efficient, sustainable, and tailored to the unique needs of each project. Our comprehensive range of services includes: Mechanical Engineering: Specializing in HVAC systems, energy management, and sustainable design, we ensure the comfort and efficiency of the spaces we work on. Electrical Engineering: We offer complete electrical system design and implementation, ensuring safety, reliability, and compliance with the latest standards. Plumbing Services: From initial design to final installation, our plumbing solutions are designed to be both efficient and environmentally friendly.

OUR SERVICES

Electrical installation

We design electrical systems to keep your power flowing safely and reliably, delivering electricity where and when you need it. We carefully calculate the load, select the right wiring and components, and design the layout to ensure efficiency and safety, so you can enjoy a well-lit, powered, and connected space every day.

Plumbing

We design plumbing systems to keep your water flowing smoothly, providing you with fresh water on demand and ensuring wastewater is efficiently carried away. We carefully calculate water pressure, select the right pipes and fixtures, and design the layout to ensure reliability and convenience, so you can enjoy a well-functioning system every day.

HVAC System

We design HVAC systems to keep you cool in summer, warm in winter, and comfortable all year round. We calculate the heat load, choose the right equipment, and ensure air flows just right, so you can breathe easy in a perfectly conditioned space.

Fire Systems

We design fire protection systems to keep you safe and secure, providing rapid response in the event of a fire. We carefully assess the building's layout, select the right detectors, alarms, and sprinklers, and design the system to ensure fast and effective coverage, so you can have peace of mind knowing you're protected around the clock.

Borehole Systems

We design borehole systems to keep your water supply reliable and sustainable, tapping into underground resources to provide clean water on demand. We carefully assess the site, select the right drilling methods and equipment, and design the system to ensure a consistent and efficient flow, so you can enjoy a dependable water source every day.

Swimming Pool

We design swimming pools to create a refreshing and enjoyable oasis, tailored to your needs and preferences. We carefully plan the pool layout, select the right filtration and circulation systems, and ensure proper water treatment and safety features, so you can relax and swim in crystal-clear water, all season long.
